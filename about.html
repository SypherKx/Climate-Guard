About ClimateGard

ClimateGard was built with a simple goal: to make gardening smarter, greener, and easier for everyone. We use data-driven insights to help users choose plants that match their specific regional climates. From hot deserts to humid jungles, our platform analyzes weather patterns and soil conditions to guide you toward plants that will thrive naturally.
